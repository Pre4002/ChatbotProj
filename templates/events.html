Events at the National Rail Museum

Explore our unique events celebrating the heritage and wonder of Indian Railways. Join us for special exhibitions, workshops, heritage rides, and more throughout the year!

Annual Railway Heritage Festival

Date: March 15 - March 20

Highlights:

  • Special exhibitions of historic locomotives
  • Guided heritage tours showcasing the evolution of Indian Railways
  • Live steam engine demonstrations and joyrides
  • Model railway displays

Steam Locomotive Showcase

Date: Monthly (First Saturday)

Highlights:

  • Experience a short ride on a real steam locomotive
  • Special presentations on steam technology and train history
  • Meet the engineers and experts who maintain these historic trains

Children’s Railway Week

Date: June 1 - June 7

Highlights:

  • Fun workshops in train model building and railway-themed arts
  • Storytime sessions about famous train journeys
  • Toy train rides and interactive displays for children

Photography and Art Competition

Date: August 15

Highlights:

  • Open to amateur and professional photographers and artists
  • Capture the spirit of Indian railways with your unique perspective
  • Winning entries displayed in the museum’s art gallery

Railway Technology Week

Date: November 12 - November 18

Highlights:

  • Learn about the evolution of railway technology
  • Explore model exhibits and technology demonstrations
  • Special talks by railway engineers and historians

Night at the Museum

Date: December 10

Highlights:

  • Exclusive night tour with special lighting and decor
  • Storytelling sessions on railway legends and historic journeys
  • Experience the magic of railways under the stars

Seasonal Festivities

  • Independence Day Celebrations – August 15

    • Flag hoisting, patriotic exhibitions, and special displays on Indian Railways' role in nation-building.

  • Republic Day Commemorations – January 26

    • Exclusive exhibitions and activities celebrating India's railway heritage.

Check back often for updates or book your tickets in advance!
